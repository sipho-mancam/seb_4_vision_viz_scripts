How Load Animations Work

Load animations simulate progress by gradually increasing a visual indicator (like a bar or percentage) from 0% to 100%. They provide user feedback during operations where the actual progress can't be precisely measured. The animation uses JavaScript to update the width of the progress bar and the percentage text at regular intervals.
